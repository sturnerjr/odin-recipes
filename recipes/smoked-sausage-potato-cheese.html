Smoked Sausage and Potato Skillet with Cheese

Description

A super simple recipe with smoked sausage and potatoes covered in cheese

  • Vegatable Oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • 1 pound smoked sausage
  • 6 large russet potatoes
  • Cheese of your choice
  1. Rinse and peel potatoes. Cut into 1/4 slices.
  2. Heat large skillet with vegetable oil over medium heat. Add potato slices and salt and pepper.
  3. While potatoes cook, slice the smoked sausage into 1/2 inch slices.
  4. Once potatoes are fork tender, add smoked sausage and heat.
  5. Once sausage is cooked to your desired doness, add cheese of your choosing and cover until the cheese is melted.
  6. Serve and enjoy!
