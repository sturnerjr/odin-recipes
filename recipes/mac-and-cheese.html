Mac and Cheese Recipe

Description

This simple mac and cheese recipe is one that your family will love

  • 5 cups milk
  • 1 lb elbow macaroni, dry
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese, or cheese of your choosing
  1. In a large pot, bring the milk to a boil
  2. Add the pasta and stir consantly until the pasta is cooked, about 10 minutes
  3. Turn off the heat, then add the shredded cheese. Stir until cheese is melted and pasta is coated evenly.
  4. Serve and enjoy!
